About Me

Hey there! Thanks for coming to my website and checking out my platform! You’ll quickly find out that I am the biggest FIMS fan. I love our program, our faculty, the opportunities, the cool resources, and the incredibly passionate and creative people. There are so many things I adore about FIMS, but we all know you have to be critical of the systems and institutions you are a part of to ensure the growth, development, and sustainability of them. That’s why I am running to be your next FIMSSC president. I am here to actively partake and listen in on conversations about your frustrations and loves about FIMS and work collaboratively with all participants to improve the FIMS student experience. Check out my platform below!

How to Vote / When to Vote

You can vote by going to the Western Elections Website on February 1 or 2 and selecting your desired candidate and submitting! It’s that simple. Make sure to tell all your friends to vote and educate them on how to vote if they do not know! Results will be released February 4, on Results Night.

Most likely, as a FIMS student you are well aware of the importance of voting and the intrinsic power structures of elections. But even if you don’t, that’s okay! That’s why we do things like student politics to better understand the whole process and environment. Voting is important to me as democracy pushes for student voices and values to be at the forefront of student governance and leadership. I believe every student should vote because exercising your rights is powerful, influential, and integral to ensuring your opinions are heard and the acts of change you desire are instigated.
